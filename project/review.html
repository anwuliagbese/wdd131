Frequently Asked Questions

Our meals are freshly prepared daily.
We offer a wide variety of balanced meals, including Local dishes, continental dishes, low-carb and dessert options. Each meal is crafted with fresh ingredients to promote healthy living.
You can place an order directly through our website by visiting the “Order Now” page. Simply select your desired meals, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout.
We provide detailed ingredient lists for each meal. You can filter our menu based on dietary restrictions, and we are happy to accommodate special requests whenever possible.

Orders can be canceled or modified up to 24 hours before the scheduled delivery time. Please contact our customer support team for assistance.
